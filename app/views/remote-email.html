Instructions for research

Ref: remote research

***Please read these instructions carefully***

Hello,
Many thanks for helping us with our research for the Department for Education.

  • Step 1: Get set up, download the app (2-5 minutes to complete)
  • Step 2: Complete the test (45 minutes)

In addition: 10 participants who took part in the research will be asked to take part in a follow-up chat with a researcher (30 minutes). We will contact you directly and you will be paid £30 for your time.

How it works

To take part, you will need to use a mobile smartphone or tablet if possible. The research session will be recorded through your device.

If you are using a laptop or desktop computer, you will need do the test through a Chrome browser. If you don't have Chrome, email fergus.roche@digital.education.gov.uk who will walk you through how to set it up.

Step 1:
Get set up, download the app

We're going to capture your feedback through an app that will record your phone screen, face video, and audio.

If you are using a smartphone or tablet:

  1. Read all these instructions before pressing the blue Start the research button below.
  2. This will prompt you to install the Participate App
  3. Install the app
  4. Follow the setup steps in the app. Make sure you allow it to access to your mic, screen, and camera
  5. Once you click "Start!" after completing the app setup, complete the research tasks below

Step 2:
Tasks to complete the test

We have created an example of what a government service might look like. This one is about retraining to get a new job. Complete the following tasks when your asked:

  1. Move through the different screens as if this was a service you were interested in
  2. Check your skills and experience
  3. Look for some training
  4. Find a job
  5. You will be asked some questions part of the way through the test and also at the end
  6. And remember to talk out loud as we're recording the session
Start the research

https://participate.lookback.io/FXB5jE

When am I done?

  • To end the session, click on the circular Participate icon (yellow hand) to the side of the screen and hit "End." Then click "Yes! Upload it!"
  • Wait for the screen that shows your uploading progress until it reaches 100% and you see a screen that says "Awesome! Your experience was uploaded!" Please do not immediately quit out of the app, since doing this will prevent the recording from uploading.

    • To access the tasks at any time, or pause the session at any point, you can click on the circular Participate icon (yellow hand) to the side of the screen. This will pause the session. Hit "Resume" to unpause.

    Compensation

    As a thank you for taking part in our research, you will be paid £30 for this research as a thank you for taking part. This research session should take no longer that 30 minutes.

    In addition, a few participants will be invited to a 30 minute interview. This also pays £30

    What is the research about

    You are helping the Government design a new training offer for adults. The Department for Education (DfE) is running research to help them understand how to enable more adults to get better jobs through training. We're looking for adults aged 24 and above.

    This is a great opportunity to genuinely influence government policy, and you do not need to have any prior knowledge or recent training experience to take part. We will simply be asking for your views and opinions. In addition, you may be asked to try out prototypes of the new service.
